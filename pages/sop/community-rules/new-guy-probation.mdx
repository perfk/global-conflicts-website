# New Guy Probation Period

*Last updated: June 22, 2024*

## Purpose of Probation

New users receive the "New Guy" role and must complete 4 weekends to earn full membership. During this period, the community reserves the right to remove access without following standard punishment procedures.

The probation serves as both an identification system and a user review period where staff assess whether individuals align with community standards.

## Review Process

The review operates passively rather than actively. Unless a player draws staff attention through rule violations or community complaints, they're presumed to be a good fit.

**Staff observe**:
- Rule adherence
- Conduct in and out of game
- Community interaction
- Gameplay health contributions
- Responsiveness to leadership

## Key Standard

> Fun is the ultimate goal...we strive for organized, well-disciplined fun.

## Failure Outcomes

If probation fails due to character concerns (poor attitude, conduct issues, inability to follow orders):

- **First failure**: 3-month ban. May rejoin and repeat probation.
- **Second failure**: 6-month ban (doubled from previous)
- **Subsequent failures**: Each ban doubles the previous length
