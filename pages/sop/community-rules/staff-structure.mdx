# Staff Structure

## Staff Roles and Responsibilities

### Admin

The highest-ranking position with root access and control over the community. Major decisions require consensus among the admin team.

**Duties**:
- Managing Discord, game servers, and Teamspeak
- Welcoming members
- Enforcing bans
- Serving as in-game admin when necessary

### Game Moderator (GM)

Moderates the playerbase both in-game and across platforms. Has access to user warning/ban logs and carries weight in internal decisions.

**Responsibilities**: Mirror admin duties but with less authority over community-wide policy.

### Public Relations Officer

Creates social media content and manages community platforms.

**Focus**:
- Engagement
- Public image
- Welcoming new users

### Mission Review Officer

Screens missions for bugs and quality before publication. Provides constructive feedback to mission creators.

### Discord & Teamspeak Moderators

Trusted members with platform-specific moderation authority.

### Instructor

Teaches training courses on Arma-related competencies to community members.

### Mission Maker

User with permissions to create and upload missions to the test server.

### Mission Tester

Volunteers who assist in testing new missions during review phases.

### Member

Earned status after participating in four weekends of community activities.

### New Guy

Entry-level designation for newly joined users before progressing to Member status.
