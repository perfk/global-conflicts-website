# Player Punishment SOPs

*Last updated: August 6, 2025*

## What Are the Infraction SOPs?

The SOPs establish a set of procedures used by staff regarding infractions and discipline. They function as guidelines with categorized violations and corresponding ban durations. Staff can escalate beyond initial penalties or issue permanent bans when situations warrant immediate action.

## Staff Roles

### Admins
- Implement bans
- Conduct duration polls
- Create appeal threads

### Game Moderators
- Issue in-game kicks and temporary bans when necessary
- Warn players

### Discord Moderators
- Ban spam/scam accounts
- Apply timeouts
- Manage slowmode
- Delete rule-violating posts

### Members
- Follow rules and report unaddressed infractions

## Key Disciplinary Procedures

### Reports
Members document infractions with evidence (recordings, screenshots). Staff may decline reports filed over one month after incidents occurred.

### Warnings
First action typically taken. Recorded without counting toward strikes but considered during punishment determination.

### Bans
Admins decide duration through 24-hour community polls using predetermined options.

- **Behavior infractions**: Take effect immediately
- **In-game bans**: Begin next session

### Appeals
Available for bans exceeding 24 hours. Admins may lift or reduce bans with ⅔ majority agreement.

## Major Infraction Categories

### Behavior Violations
Includes harassment, doxxing, discrimination, gore/pornography posting, spamming, and terms-of-service breaches.

**Duration**: 1 week to permanent

###  In-Game Violations
Encompasses:
- Teamkilling
- Disruptive voice communications
- Unauthorized asset use
- Chain-of-command violation
- Incompetence in specialized roles

**Duration**: 1 week to 1 month
